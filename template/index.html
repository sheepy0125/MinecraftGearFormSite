Sheepy's God Gear Shop




The God Gear will cost:

  • The cost to make the gear in diamonds, or already made gear
  • 15 diamonds per elytra
  • 5 diamonds per order (profit)
Some things to note:
  • You will only have to pay for the diamonds to make the gear (you will not have to pay 2 sticks or iron for example).
  • If you wish to purchase other than diamond gear, you will have to drop off the gear (for example gold armor or a turtle helmet).
  • You can only order up to 5 of a paid item and 3 of a free item at a time on the form (if you wish to order more, you will have to make a new form).
